How to Play

When you first run the game, you will encounter a language picker. Click on the language in which you want to run the game.

Once you get past the intro sequence and all that jazz, you will be free to control Cubert to your liking. Click on things with the left mouse button to interact with them, and the right mouse button to examine them. If you want to bring up your collection of inventory items, click anywhere else on the screen with the right mouse button.

Alternatively, you can also bring up a handy-dandy menu by pressing ESC, which allows you to load, save, resume, or *gasp* quit. Bear in mind that you can access this menu anytime, I repeat, anytime in the game.
